What We Offer

Solutions Built Around Your Operation

From a single reception desk to a multi-site enterprise campus — every solution below is modular and can be deployed standalone or combined into one unified system.

☁️

Unified Cloud Platform

One dashboard to manage every device, every site and every user — access logs, alerts and reports updated in real time, accessible from anywhere.

  • Live device health monitoring across all locations
  • Role-based access for admins, managers & security staff
  • Automated alerts for tailgating, forced entry & tamper events
  • Exportable compliance & audit reports
    • 🕒

    Time Attendance

    Biometric clock-in/out synced directly with payroll — no manual entry, no buddy-punching.

  • Face fingerprint based attendance
  • Shift, overtime leave rules engine
  • Payroll-software export (Excel, API, ERP)
    • 🪪

    Visitor Management

    Pre-registration, instant badge printing and host notification — a professional front desk experience for every guest.

  • QR / OTP based pre-approval
  • ID scanning & auto photo capture
  • Host SMS / email notification on arrival
    • 🚧

    Perimeter & Access Control

    Turnstiles, flap barriers and boom gates integrated with biometric or card credentials at every entry point.

  • Anti-tailgating & anti-passback logic
  • Fire-alarm triggered fail-safe release
  • Multi-door, multi-zone scheduling
    • 🚗

    Parking Management

    ANPR-based vehicle entry/exit with automated billing and reserved-slot enforcement for staff & visitors.

  • Number plate recognition at boom barriers
  • Pre-paid & subscription parking plans
  • Real-time occupancy & slot-availability display
    • 📹

    Integrated Surveillance

    CCTV and ANPR feeds layered directly onto the same dashboard as your access control events for unified investigation.

  • Event-linked video playback (door open + camera clip)
  • Remote viewing via mobile app
  • 30/60/90-day cloud or NVR storage options
    • One Platform

    Manage Everything From One Screen

    Every solution above runs on the same ENHANCE AI SYSTEM cloud platform — whether you're securing a single branch or a 100-site network. Add new devices, locations and users without touching a single line of code.

    Available as a web dashboard for admins and a mobile app for on-the-go approvals, alerts and reporting.
